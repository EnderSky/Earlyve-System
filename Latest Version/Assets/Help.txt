Instructions to Enter Student Data
1. Select appropriate header "Early Leave" or "Latecomers" followed by "Enter Student Data"
2. Click the cursor in the "Student ID" text field.
3. Scan the barcode on the student's ID   OR   Enter the student's ID manually.
4. Fill up the "Reason" text field.
5. Click on the submit button.

Instructions to Query Data
1. Select the appropriate menu header "Early Leave" or "Latecomers" followed by "Query by Student ID" or "Query by Date"
2. Click the cursor in the text field.
3. Enter the appropriate data (student ID or date).
4. Click the submit button.
